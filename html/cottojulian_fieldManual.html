Battle Tactics

The following maneuver is called a flank.
If an enemy is in-between you and the circle, it is a great way to gain the upper-hand.
One to three players can remain engaged with the enemy to maintain their attention.
One to three players will move around (flank) the enemy to a position of advantage.
The flanking force will then engage the enemy from the side or rear.
The maneuver is complete when the Engaging & Flanking Forces make adjacent passes of the enemy position.
